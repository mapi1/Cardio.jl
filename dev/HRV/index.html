An addaptive filter for HRV data Based on: Wessel, N., Voss, A., Malberg, H., Ziehmann, Ch., Voss, H. U., Schirdewan, A., Meyerfeldt, U.,Kurths, J.: Nonlinear analysis of complex phenomena in cardiological data, Herzschr. Elektrophys., 11(3), 2000, 159-173, doi:10.1007/s003990070035.
